# Resources

External links worth keeping. The official docs are the source of truth for exact
function names; this vault is the "why" and the Ableton bridge.

## Official Strudel

- **Workshop / getting started** — https://strudel.cc/workshop/getting-started/
  (do this once, hands-on; it's short and well-paced.)
- **Function reference** — https://strudel.cc/learn/ (every function, searchable).
- **Sounds list** — https://strudel.cc/learn/sounds/
- **Effects** — https://strudel.cc/learn/effects/
- **Synths** — https://strudel.cc/learn/synths/
- **Mini-notation** — https://strudel.cc/learn/mini-notation/
- **Tonal (scales/chords)** — https://strudel.cc/learn/tonal/
- **Signals** — https://strudel.cc/learn/signals/
- **Technical manual (embedding, packages, REPL internals)** — https://strudel.cc/technical-manual/

## Background / concepts

- **TidalCycles** (the parent language; Strudel is its JS port) — https://tidalcycles.org/
  Tidal docs often explain a concept more deeply; most ideas map directly.
- **Strudel blog** — https://strudel.cc/blog/

## Community & inspiration

- The **Strudel Discord** (linked from strudel.cc) — patches, help, feedback.
- Search YouTube for "Strudel live coding" / "TidalCycles" for full-track walkthroughs
  (like the `dmb-yt-bite.js` reference in this project).
- **switch-angel** — the artist whose prelude is in `patterns/apx/switch-angel/`; her
  scripts are a deep well of advanced custom words (see [[15 This Sandbox#prelude]]).
